I am a PhD student in the <a href="https://mit.whoi.edu/" title="https://mit.whoi.edu/" target="_blank">MIT-WHOI Joint Program</a> where I study physical oceanography with my advisors <a href="https://www2.whoi.edu/staff/mspall/" title="https://www2.whoi.edu/staff/mspall/" target="_blank">Dr. Michael Spall</a> and <a href="https://ilebras.github.io/" title="https://ilebras.github.io/" target="_blank">Dr. Isabela Le Bras</a>. My research focuses on large-scale Arctic Ocean physical oceanography—particularly the dynamics of the Beaufort Gyre in the Arctic's Canadian Basin—but I also have interests in sea ice, glacier-ocean interactions, and Antarctic physical oceanography.
